About me

Maximilian Meyer

I am a German software enthusiast currently located in the region Stuttgart.

Software is my greatest passion, but I also like the whole STEM spectrum. As a lifelong learner, I enjoy reading or thinking about chemistry, mathematics and technology. Besides my favorite topics I like to learn new things about other topics. Especially when I can develop software for it. This is one of the main reasons why I like software engineering because so I can constantly expand my knowledge.

I am familiar with programming languages like C/C++, Java, TypeScript and Python. Recently I worked with frameworks like Angular, Node.js, Spring Boot and ASP.NET. MySQL, Microsoft SQL Server and DB2 are database management systems that I have worked with in the past. Linux and Windows are the operating systems that I use for development.

When I am not coding or thinking about stuff you can find me practicing my running pace at my favorite place or occasionally I am busy saving a "galaxy far, far away..." in a video game.

At the moment I am searching new challenges. So, I am open for project offers for work, free time or open source.
